Very first sketchwalk! In this one we visited the beautiful Giardini Margherita and painted 3 different locations for about 45 minutes each one... quite the challenge! specially if you, like I was, are not used to sketching outside with time limits.

I joined the [Urban Sketchers Bologna](https://www.facebook.com/groups/37592867254) group... you should join your city chapter too, it's fantastic!
